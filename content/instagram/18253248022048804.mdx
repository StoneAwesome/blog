    It was love at first sight when we saw this project. Look how by pulling the sinks in this commercial bathroom away from the wall and creating a showpiece of an island is creating a hub... a meeting place, a conversation. How magical is it just by pulling something off of the wall you automatically make it more social? Don't be afraid to play with the footprint of a space, throw out the rules and create something truly unique. And of course, use natural stone as often as you can, there is no better artist in the world!
