    Material: Ice Jade Marble (also called cold jade marble and green primavera)

    Renovated in 2020 this Belgium restaurant oozes sophistication yet at the same time has a playful side and doesn't come across as pretentious. Perhaps due to the curved sofa dining option but we like to think it also had to do with the stone selection. Definitely, a unique selection for a marble countertop the choice to go with this multi-color and dimensional slab adds interest and a splash of drama to the space without distracting from the rest of the interiors.
