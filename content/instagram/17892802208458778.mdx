    Sometimes Arabescato marble is considered a compromise between Carrara and Calacatta due to its strength and price range. However, it is its use here in both a kitchen and bath application definitely shows why it should be sought out first! It has beautiful dusty grey expressive veining that dances across a clean white background. It provides that statement piece moment while still remaining classic.⠀
