    Title: Lakeshore

    Material: Calacatta Tucci Marble counters and ceramic tile


    This @chadcalebdorsey designed kitchen and dining area is all about the details. From the backless upper cabinets that let the texture from the ceramic tiles shine through to the joinery of the countertop slabs. Usually, we see an effort to disguise when two slabs are brought together to give the illusion of a thicker one however here there is a defined step from one to the other referred to as a quirk miter. It is a small detail but has a big design impact and we love it!


    Think big even on the small stuff!
