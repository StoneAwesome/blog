    Despite the somewhat misleading name super white quartzite is a beautiful material. Used here by Tom Robertson he is able to contrast it with both black, in a study area, as well as stark white and warm wood in the kitchen. The designer shows off the different sides of this natural stone’s personality creating visual interest from floor to ceiling.
